How to Scan Your Bracelet

  1. Lock your device.
  2. Bring the bracelet’s logo near your device’s NFC reader.
  3. Unlock your device — it should scan immediately.

Android Users

For Android users, please ensure that the NFC feature is enabled on your device before proceeding.

Waterproofing Recommendations

While our bracelets are designed to be water-resistant, we do not recommend the following:

  • Wearing the bracelet while showering
  • Placing the bracelet in the washing machine
  • Submerging the bracelet in water for extended periods of time

For the best care and longevity of your bracelet, please avoid these conditions.
